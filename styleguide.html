Usage tips

Keeping it consistent

  • Use chunky margins and generous spacing—let components breathe.
  • Stack content inside bold borders and drop shadows to sell the brutalist vibe.
  • Reserve the yellow accent for calls-to-action and animated details.
  • Always provide a contrasting fallback color when placing type over imagery.
